I will write a paragraph for each methodology of SDLC. There are many of them but I will suggest a few. My suggestions and reasons are as follows:

  • A: Agile Methodology - Highly adaptive and iterative, suitable for dynamic environments.
  • B: Waterfall Methodology - Best for projects with clear requirements and well-defined stages.
  • C: Spiral Model - Combines iterative development with risk assessment, ideal for complex projects.

Among so many SDLC Methodologies, the ones that are in the Agile classification are better. Among Agile SDLC Methods, I have also a few suggestions. My suggestions and reasons are as follows:

  • A: Scrum - Focuses on delivering small, incremental updates frequently, promoting teamwork and adaptability.
  • B: Kanban - Great for visualizing workflows and reducing bottlenecks in development processes.
  • C: Extreme Programming (XP) - Emphasizes high-quality code through continuous feedback and testing.
  • D: Lean Development - Optimizes efficiency by eliminating waste and focusing on delivering value quickly.
